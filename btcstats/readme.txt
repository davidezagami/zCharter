btcbot.py
_______________________________________________________________________________________________________
Simulate trading on any pair, any exchange, any time period.
Strategy is customizable.
Default strategy is buy on monday, then sell within end of the week at 5% profit,
under the popular myth that prices are lowest on mondays. (This is indeed a myth,
but works over certain time periods simply because, in hindsight, cryptocurrencies
had regular price increases over most time periods in the past years.)
_______________________________________________________________________________________________________
USAGE: 	download the market data of interest and save it in CSVs folder, in CSV format.
		launching btcbot.py will simulate for all CSVs found, and save results in botplots folder.
		botlogs folder contains logs for every action taken in the simulations.

#######################################################################################################

btcstats.py
_______________________________________________________________________________________________________
Visualize periodic price correlations on any pair, any exchange, any time period.
Analysis is customizable.
Default analysis is, with respect to open price on monday, show, on average over the
whole time period, relative price distributions over the week. (This debunks the popular
myth that prices are lowest on mondays, since starting the analysis on a different day
of the week produces identical results. However a clear correlation that prices are lowest
on the first day can be seen simply because, in hindsight, cryptocurrencies had regular
price increases over most time periods in the past years.)
_______________________________________________________________________________________________________
USAGE: 	download the market data of interest and save it in CSVs folder, in CSV format.
		launching btcstats.py will run analysis for all CSVs found, and save results in statsplots folder.
